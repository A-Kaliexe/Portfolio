About Me

I am a creative frontend developer. I am passionate in catering my works for ----- that make an impact, inspire and connect with people.

When I am not developing I enjoy spending times reading some great books, playing games, or finding hidden gem restaurants.

Hey there! I'm Kali. My journey into the world of coding began in an unexpected place: Myspace. It feels like ages ago now, but that's where I unknowingly took my first steps into the realm of coding. Playing around with customizing my profile sparked something inside me. As I continued through high school, I found myself drawn to writing more and more lines of HTML and CSS code. It was like unlocking a new language, one that allowed me to bring my creative visions to life on the screen. Despite my initial inclination towards a career in health, coding kept calling out to me. Years later, when I stumbled upon The Odin Project and GCode House, I saw it as a way to fill my spare time with something I enjoyed. Little did I know, it would ignite a fire within me. The journey through The Odin Project wasn't just about learning to code; it was about discovering a passion I didn't know I had. Each line of code I wrote felt like a piece of myself coming alive, a puzzle I couldn't wait to solve. Since then, I've thrown myself into mastering programming languages like JavaScript. It's been a journey of self-discovery and growth, one that has brought me here, eager to share my skills and make a meaningful contribution to your business.
